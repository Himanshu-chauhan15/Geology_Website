VAGRECHA PLAST CHEM

We are pleased to introduce ourselves as manufacturer and supplier of various minerals and chemicals used in Paint, Polymer, refractory, Ceramics, Steel, Pharmaceuticals, Cosmetics, Soap, and Food industries.
